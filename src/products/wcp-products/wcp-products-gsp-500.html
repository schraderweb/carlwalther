GSP 500

The next-generation target pistol for the aspiring and elite marksman. Evolving a legendary platform, the GSP 500 incorporates advanced materials and refined ergonomics while upholding the core principle of modular "Unit" construction.

Features include a new Match Ultralight trigger system with a digitally adjustable release point (250g to 1500g), a redesigned ergonomic grip in high-grade composite with fully modular side panels and palm shelf (XS-XXL), and the tool-free caliber interchangeability system, now compatible with .22 LR, .32 S&W Long, and the new .22 Short Match kit. Uncompromising reliability, sub-10mm precision at 25 meters, and optimal balance define its performance. The low-profile optic-ready bridge and integrated weight system make it the most adaptable GSP ever. Each pistol comes with three magazines and a custom-fit grip tool.

GSP 500 Professional includes a hard Anodized Aluminum Flight Case, the internal recoil dampening system, and an additional set of precision balance weights. (drawing)

See accessories link at the bottom of the page for caliber conversion kits, specific weight sets, and performance triggers.
