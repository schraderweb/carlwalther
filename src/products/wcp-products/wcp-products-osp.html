OSP

The OSP was designed for the Olympic rapid fire event. Not only does it excel at this event, it is the perennial winner.
Features include a Morini grip, which places the bore at a lower level to reduce recoil. The barrel has an integral weight, and it's ported to virtually eliminate recoil. It comes with a 100-200g trigger.
The Modular Construction allows one to change calibers in seconds.
