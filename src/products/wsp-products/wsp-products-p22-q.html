P22

The .22 rimfire handgun will never look the same. The P22 changed everything. Walther adapted the tactical styling and Performance Design concepts from our centerfire handguns and applied them to the .22 L.R. This created a new movement in the design of contemporary rimfire handguns ... and an explosion of imitators. As a stylish, fun gun to own, the P22 is at home on the shooting range, around the camp or in a museum of modern art. 1. Threaded barrel. 2. Low profile three dot polymer combat sights. 3. Loaded chamber indicator. 4. Front and rear slide serrations. 5. Nickel or matte black slide. 6. External slide stop. 7. 3 safeties. 8. Two magazine styles. 9. Ergonomic Walther grip. 10. Paddle-style ambidextrous magazine release lever. 11. Double/single action trigger. 12. Checkered trigger guard. 13. Picatinny accessory rail.
