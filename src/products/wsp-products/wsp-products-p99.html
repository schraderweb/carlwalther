P99

The P 99 is the latest double action design from Walther.
It features a polymer frame, high capacity magazine, loaded chamber indicator, decocking button, ambidextrous magazine release, changeable backstrap, frame accessory rail, and a new "Anti-stress trigger mechanism".
The P 990 is the DAO version. (Double Action Only)
Available calibers include 9mm (a.k.a. 9mm NATO, 9mm Luger, 9mm Parabellum and 9x19) 9x21mm .40S&W
