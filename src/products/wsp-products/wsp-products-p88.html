P88

The P 88 was designed for the world police market and is the finest 9mm handgun ever produced.
The P 88 is insensitive to variations in ammunition. Its the only Walther made that is completely ambidextrous.
Its double action and integral safeties combined with a high capacity magazine make it superior to everything previously made.
