P22 Target

The P22 Target uses the same technology as the P22 standard.
The barrel length is now 127 mm instead of 87 mm. Thus the line of sight is extended by 40 mm. The longer line of sight allows for more concentrated aiming during target shooting and because of that better scores can be achieved.
The P22 Target is available in caliber .22 l.r. with 127 mm barrel and barrel weight.
The P22 Target "First Edition" comes in a special plastic case and includes both the 87mm and 127mm barrels.
Only 1000 "First Edition" models will be made
