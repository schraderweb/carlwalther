PP

PP "Last Edition"
For seventy years the German made Walther PP's / PPK's have been considered to be the handguns of choice for law enforcement, personal use and collectors.
As thank you for our customers we have designed the last 500 Walther PP's, made in Germany, as commemorative "Last Editions".
Each gun has a high polish "Walther blue" and is engraved with "Last Edition" and the years "1929 - 1999" on the slide.
Included with the gun will be a 150 minutes video "Walther a German Legend" highlighting the history, weapons, and interesting aspects from the last 110 years of Walther, a special gun case with an engraved brass plate depicting the PP / PPK history, and a certificate that this gun is one of the last 500 to be produced in Ulm, Germany.
