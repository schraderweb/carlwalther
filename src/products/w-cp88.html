CP88

The first 8-shot Walther CO2 pistol.
The design and function of this newly developed weapon match the legendary Walther P 88.
The CP 88 pistol has an interchangeable barrel system for versatility. It's designed for sporting use like the "Falling Target".
The 8-shot cylindrical magazine and the power cocking system for the CO2 cartridges ensure quick and safe firing.

Walther once again sets the standard. Using the most advanced manufacturing methods and excellent workmanship guaranteeing superb quality.
