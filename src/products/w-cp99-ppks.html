cp99

The second 8-shot Walther CO2 pistol. The design and function of this newly developed weapon match the Walther P 99. It's designed for sporting use like the "Falling Target" or plinking. The 8-shot cylindrical magazine and the power cocking system for the CO2 cartridges ensure quick and safe firing. Walther once again sets the standard. Features include a replaceable magazine that can even be removed without discharging the CO2. A weaver accessory rail under the fore-frame. Single or double action trigger. Shipped in a foam lined plastic case.
