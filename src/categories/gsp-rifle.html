The GSP Match and Super Match rifle conversion units Technical Data

Manufacturers: Carl Walther GmbH. Ulm, Germany and Earl's Repair Service, Inc. Tewksbury, MA.
Action: GSP pistol (.22 LR)
Caliber:  .22 LR (5.6 mm)
Barrel: Standard: S/S Match grade with six flutes and threaded compensator.
Super Match: S/S Match grade without fluting or compensator.
Twist:  1 in 16?"(425 mm) (exclusive progressive design)
Barrel length: 16?" (425 mm),18?" (465 mm) with GSP Rifle compensator
Crown: Earl's special design (patented)
Sights: Optics (aimpoint, ultradot, etc.) (no machining) or optional scope mounting (machining required)
Magazine Capacity: 5-shot, 8-shot, and now 10-shot capacity.
Trigger Pull Weight: 1 to 3 lbs. (454 to 1362 grams) (adjustable)
Weight: 8.55 lbs. (3.88 kg) (complete rifle without scope)
Overall Length: 35?" (908 mm) Match
36?" (933 mm) Super Match
Stock: Laminated wood. Standard Kit in black, brown or multicolor (R/H only)
Match & Super Match kit in "Walther blue" and black (R/H or L/H) with special logo and adjustable butt plate
